Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church

Our Mission:

The mission of our church is to equip the congregation to be witnesses to the Kingdom of God and to live the gospel of Jesus Christ, so that we may reach out to friends and neighbors, to acquaintances and strangers, of local, state, national, and world communities, showing them the love of God through our actions. To accomplish this the church challenges and supports its members and friends to grow and mature in a Christ-like manner in their relationships with God and all people.
