Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church

Adult Ministries:

Here at the Dover Foxcroft Congregational there are a variety of ways for adults to get involved and to learn and grow as people of faith. From fellowship groups, to book clubs and craft circles, there is something for everybody and new opportunities are coming up all the time.

We just completed a timely adult ed series on the Reformation, here on the 500th anniversary. We made interesteing observations about things then and things now. Martin Luther and John Calvin lived in very different times from today, and we had good discussions about what carries forward to today and what does not. While the notion of "salvation through faith alone" is foundational to Christianity and Protestantism in particular, we all agreed that faith along with "good works" captures the full expression of discipleship to Christ.

Up next (dates TBA), Pastor Steve will be leading us in a series about Creation, Christianity, and the Ecological Crisis as a spiritual and moral challenge to us.
