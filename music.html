Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church

Music:

We have a long history here at the Congregational Church of supporting and celebrating the arts. This is evident in the individual lives of our members but in our own programs as well. Music is a prominent part of our life together and we are very thankful to those who share their gifts.

Church Choir

Here at the Congregational Church we are blessed with a wonderful choir. The choir is about 15 voices strong and rehearses both before and after Sunday worship. If you would like to see and hear our church choir visit our vimeo site and watch a recent worship service. During the summer months the choir takes a break but we are blessed with a number of individuals and small ensembles willing to offer their gifts of music instead.

Bell Club

The sound of a bell choir is something very special. We currently have a group of 'ringers' meeting informally for fun and fellowship. All are invited. For more information as well as dates and times contact Chris Paydos the church adminstrator.

Special Music

At the Congregational Church there are always opportunities to share your musical gifts. Solists and ensembles are welcomed to offer anthems, offertories, preludes, postludes, etc. Those interested should contact Julie Knowlton.
