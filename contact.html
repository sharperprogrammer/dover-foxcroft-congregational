Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church

Contact Us:

Whether you are a local resident or are exploring our church from afar, we would love to hear from you and serve you in any way we can.

If you would like to visit us in person, we are very easy to find. You will find us just off Monument Square in Dover Foxcroft. Our physical address is 828 West Main Street in Dover Foxcroft, ME (Route 15). Look for the big white steeple and clock. You can't miss us.

General inquiries can be directed to the church's administrative assistant, Chris Paydos. If you would prefer to call, our phone number is 564-2872.

Our regular office hours are Tuesday through Thursday 8:00am-1:00pm.

If you would like to contact with a specific member of our staff, please visit the staff page.
