Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church

Youth:

Here at the Dover Foxcroft Congregational Church we are committed to investing in the life of our young people, celebrating their presence and involvement in the church and community. Through our words and example and with God's help, we seek to nurture them toward an active and dynamic relationship with God through Jesus Christ.

Sunday School

We currently offer Sunday School for ages PK through 8th grade and offer nursery care for younger children. Sunday School meets, September through June, during our worship hour (9:30-10:30). Please note, however, that there is no Sunday School on the first Sunday of the month. Instead families are invited to worship and recieve the sacrament of communion together.

Youth Group

The Youth Group of the church serves students grades 6 - 12. It is an intentionally Christian community of youth learning and growing through fellowship, study, service, and worship.
